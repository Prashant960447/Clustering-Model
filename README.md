# Clustering-Model
IPL franchise owner Mrs. Neeta Ambani, excited with recent IPL win, want to devise a mathematical formula to identify the player and its characteristics. So as to help her with choosing the right players during auction. Mumbai Indians team is most successful as well as spends most of money too. She wishes to reduce the spending on player selection. 
Player selection is complex method. Getting desired player is not always possible. At auction, competitors can place false bids to increase the price of desired player. In order to avoid such traps, having multiple options ready for 1 position is very important. 
Based upon the previous IPL performance, create the groups of players to be ready at auction.
